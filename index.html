SITE INTENT

Victoria Weiss provides legal services for Southeast GA. These services include Bankruptcy, Injuries and, Family Practice. Victoria has been practicing law for 28 years. She works long hours to ensure client satisfaction and has a track record showing herself to be a very competent lawyer.

CLIENT GOALS

Create a better web presence for the purpose of advertisment. Creation of a better overall design along with organization of information without overwhelming visitors.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Sean Salentino

Sean is a 23 year old Naval mechanic and, father of 2 who has been served with divorce papers. He travels a lot, and feels that his rights as a father should be protected. He wants to ensure that when he is not deployed, he is able to spend an appropriate amount of time with his children. Additionally he would like to have special arrangements for video visitation while he is deployed. Sean would come to the site. Review the family issues section. On review of the family issues section he would then want to know how to contact the law office for review of options.

Christina Mceldrew

Christina is 50 years old, and had been a HR Manager for a local telephone company for 20 years. She recently reached a point, after being unable to find comparable work, where she has to declare bankruptcy. She has some idea of what the end result of such a decision would be but needs help with the legal documentation. She also wants to be sure that she does not give up any rights or protections that she is entitled to due to ignorance of the law. Christina would come to the site, and review the Bankruptcy section. She would then determine if she wanted to contact the law office for consult.
